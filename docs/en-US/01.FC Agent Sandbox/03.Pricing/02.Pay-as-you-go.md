Under the pay-as-you-go model, charges are based on the sandbox specifications, runtime state, and actual usage duration. After a sandbox is created in FC Agent Sandbox, it can be in the active, light hibernation, deep hibernation, or destroyed state. Different states incur charges for different resources.

## Billing Granularity

- Billing granularity: Charges are calculated by the second, and usage shorter than 1 second is billed as 1 second.
- Billing cycle: Charges are settled once per hour.

## Subscription Plans

During the preview, FC Agent Sandbox describes billing rules based on three subscription plans: Eco, Std, and Pro. Different plans support different state capabilities. The plans that can be activated are subject to the preview scope.

| Subscription Plan | Applicable Scenarios | Active | Deep Hibernation | Light Hibernation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Eco | Individual developers, startup teams, and low-cost validation | Supported | Not supported | Not supported |
| Std | Enterprise production workloads | Supported | Supported | Not supported |
| Pro | Large-scale production workloads | Supported | Supported | Supported |

## Billing Rules

### Active State

When a sandbox is in the active state, you are charged for vCPU, memory, and disk. Disk usage includes a free allowance of 15 GiB.

```text
Fee = vCPU fee + memory fee + disk fee
Billable disk size = max(0, disk size - 15) GiB
```

### Light Hibernation State

After a sandbox enters light hibernation, vCPU charges stop. You are charged only for memory and disk. Disk usage includes a free allowance of 15 GiB.

```text
Fee = memory fee + disk fee
Billable disk size = max(0, disk size - 15) GiB
```

Light hibernation is supported only by the Pro plan.

### Deep Hibernation State

After a sandbox enters deep hibernation, vCPU and memory charges stop. You are charged only for disk based on the disk capacity occupied during deep hibernation. The 15 GiB free disk allowance does not apply during deep hibernation.

```text
Deep hibernation disk usage = memory size × 2 + disk size
Fee = deep hibernation disk usage × disk unit price × duration
```

### Destroyed State

After a sandbox is destroyed, no additional FC Agent Sandbox instance charges are incurred. Deep hibernation is not the same as destruction, and disk charges still apply during deep hibernation.

## Overdue Payment and Resource Release

- Pay-as-you-go charges are typically settled once per hour. If your account balance is insufficient and an overdue payment occurs, the system sends an overdue payment notification.
- After an overdue payment occurs, FC Agent Sandbox instances may be automatically stopped and no longer provide runtime capabilities.
- The retention period, release time, and recovery method for stopped sandbox data are subject to console notifications, cost and billing statements, preview activation terms, or official release announcements.

We recommend that you destroy sandboxes that are no longer needed and configure appropriate timeouts for production workloads to avoid unexpected charges caused by idle resources.

## FAQ

### Will I still be charged after a sandbox is destroyed?

No additional FC Agent Sandbox instance charges are incurred after a sandbox is destroyed. Deep hibernation is not the same as destruction, and disk charges still apply during deep hibernation.

### Is the free disk allowance shared across sandboxes?

No. Each sandbox independently includes a 15 GiB free disk allowance, which is available only in the active and light hibernation states.

### How do I view usage and bills?

During the preview, you can view usage details and bills for activated resources on Alibaba Cloud cost and billing pages. The available entry points may vary based on the preview activation scope of your account.
